Siddhi Vinayak Temple

Panchama Siddhi Vinayak Temple and Lord Ganesh temple are situated at Panchama village and it is 11 km distance from Berhampur Town. Lord Ganesh is worshipped by People also called Bata Ganesh in Siddhi Vinayak Temple. Here the lord resides in the groove of a large Pipal tree. A big gathering takes place on Ganesh puja day. Panchama Ganesh Temple is One of the best temples and most peaceful temples in Berhampur. 

A small Garden is there inside the temple. Besides this, the temple of Shiva and Hanuman temple is here and worshipped by the local people. A large number of devotees gather at this temple on Wednesdays and Vinayak Chaturthi (Ganesh Puja). Panchama Siddhi Vinayak Temple is the best tourist attraction place in Berhampur, Ganjam, Odisha.
