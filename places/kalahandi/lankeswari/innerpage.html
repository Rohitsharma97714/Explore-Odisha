Lankeshwari Temple

Lankeswari Temple is situated at Junagarh, and It is 15 km distance from Bhawanipatna towards the Southwest. Goddess Lankeswari is the presiding deity of this temple. The statue of the Maa Lankeshwari is made of black chlorite stone. Lankeshwari Temple is one of the famous Shakti Pitha of Odisha. Khandabasa Jatra at Lankeswari Pitha of Junagarh is the Main festival of the Kalahandi district in the month of Aswini. 

This goddess is also worshipped in the form of Vanudurga. There is a Kali image in front of Maa Lankeshwari on the temple premises, which is very attractive. Sri Rama Navami, Mahavishuba Sankranti & Durgapuja are the main functions of the Maa Lankeswari. Lankeswari Temple is one of the famous temples in Kalahandi, Odisha.
