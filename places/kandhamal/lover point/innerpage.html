Lover Point

Lovers Point is located at a distance of 13 km from Daringbadi and about 87 km away from Phulbani town. There are a few natural cave formations in its vicinity. This place is surrounded by dense forests and hills. The water of the spring is flowing over the rocks. The adventure of the waterfall and the environment is enjoyable. It is a very good place for a weekend holiday picnic. The natural environment is very calm and peaceful here. There is enough parking place available near Lovers’ Point. 

Lovers’ Point Forest is a popular picnic spot located inside Serene Doluri River Valley. The waterfall produces such clean and cool water, which is breathtaking to take a bath in the natural shower. The place is full of natural beauty and a good place for nature lovers. Daringbadi Lovers Point is one of the best tourist and picnic spots in the Kandhamal District, Odisha.
