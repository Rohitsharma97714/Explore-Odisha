Siddheswar Temple

The Siddheswar Temple is on the right bank of river Vaitarani. It has located 7 km from Jajpur town. It consists of Rekha Deula and Pidha Jaganmohan within a specious enclosure wall. A votive Temple depicting four incarnations of Vishnu, Shiva Linga with star-shaped Yoni Pitha are a major attraction. Besides images of Astikajaratkar, Lakulisha, Uma-Mahesvara, Mithuna figures affixed in the compound wall are of immense archaeological importance.
