Wel Come To Jajpur

Jajpur is famous for Biraja kshetra and is the headquarters of the Jajpur District. Long Long ago about 500 AD to 950 AD it was the capital of Odisha under the Kesari dynasty. Jajpur is the Shaktipitha of Odisha. There is Shiv temple of Trilochan, Akhandeleswar and Agneswar Navigay is a sacred place and another important place of Jaipur and Dasaswamedhaghat on the bank of the river Baitarani Nadi(River) and the image of Saptamatruka holds the good name of the tour place. Goddess maa Viraja is the presiding deity of Jaipur. Jajpur is honoured for its natural resources, mines and industries. This is a famous tourist place in Jajpur. Jajpur is famous for Biraja Khetra.

The Jajpur District covers an area of 2887 sq. Km. As per the 2011 census, the total population of the District is 18,26,275 with the total male population as 9,26,034 and female population as 9,01,158. The Jajpur District has 1 subdivision, 1781 Villages, 311 Gram Panchayats, 10 Tahasils and 10 Blocks, 2 Municipality, 2 N.A.C, 7 Assembly Constituency and 15 Police stations are there in Jajpur District.
