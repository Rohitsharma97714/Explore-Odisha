Silver city Boat club

Cuttack is popularly known as the Silver City of India. To preserve the name Cuttack, the Silver City Boat Club is located near the Bali Jatra grounds on the banks of the river Mahanadi with the help of Odisha Tourism and OTDC. Its purpose is that city dwellers can spend some quality time here with their families and children. The main objective of the project is to keep multiple water sports operators and promote water-based tourism in Cuttack and nearby districts. Multipurpose room, watersports, food court, cafeteria, toilet complex, restaurant, play area, Bistro, and boating facilities are available at Silver City Boat Club. Apart from this, the boat club also has a children’s park and water fountain.

There are many water sports activities like speed boats, water scooters, and pedal boats are available between 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. One of the best times to visit the Cuttack Silver City Boat Park is during the Bali Jatra festival and Kartik Purnima. The view at sunset is so beautiful that a selfie point has been set up for visitors, and this is the best place for photography. There is a floating restaurant in the middle of the river where one can enjoy the view with delicious food. One of the best times to visit the Mahanadi river bank in Cuttack is during the Balijatra festival and Kartik Purnima. Silver City Boat Club bike parking price is Rs.10 and the Car price is Rs.20.
