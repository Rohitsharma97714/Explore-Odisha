Baba Shyam Temple

Shyam baba mandir is situated in Bhatli and is 20 km away from Bargarh Town. Sri Shyam baba idol of the temple. Besides the there is a Hanuman, Siva, Radha Krishna and maa Durga temple inside the campus. The temple is a calm and peaceful place. A beautiful Shyam Kund and garden is there, which is enjoyable for visitors. The infrastructure of the temple and the interior glass work is Amazing. The main celebration of this temple is observed just 8-9 days before the festival of Holi. Bhatli Shyam baba Temple is one of the tourist places in Bargarh, Odisha.
