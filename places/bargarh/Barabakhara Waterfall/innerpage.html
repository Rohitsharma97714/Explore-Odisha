Barabakhara Waterfall

Barabakhara Waterfall is located at Ambavhana in the Barapahad hill range. It is around 47 km from Bargarh town. The Waterfall is covered with hills and dense forest. Veer Surendra Sai hidden in Bara Bakhra Caves. Barabakhara cave is associated with the history of Veer Surendra Sai. The water from the Barabakhara waterfall flows through the hills and flows through two waterfalls. The beautiful natural environment and the wondrous sound of the waterfall attract the visiter during winter. 

The place is full of natural beauty and is a good place for nature lovers. The waterfall produces such clean and cool water, which is very stupendous to take a bath in the natural shower. Bara Bakhra waterfall and Caves are one of the most popular tourist places in Bargarh, Odisha

Opening Time

Barabakhara opening time is Morning 8 am to Evening 5 pm

Best Time to visit

The best time to visit Bara Bakhara waterfall is between months of September to March.

Nearest Bus Stop

The nearest Bus stop is Ambabhona, which is located around 20 Km distance from Bara Bakhra Caves and Waterfall

Nearest Railway Station

The Nearest Railway Station is Bargarh, which is located around 48 Km distance from Bara Bakhra Caves and Waterfall

Nearest Airport

The nearest Airport is Biju Patnaik International Airport, which is located around 357 Km distance from Bara Bakhra Caves and Waterfall and 151 km from Veer Surendra Sai Airport, Jharsuguda
