Kendrapada

Wel Come To Kendrapada

Kendrapara is the district headquarter. it is a 60 km distance from Cuttack via Jagatpur on Cuttack Chandbali road. There are many popular tourist places in Kendrapara District. One famous spot is ‘Bhitara Kanika National Park’, Buddhist Shrine, Udaya Giri, and Ratna Giri. Rasabali Mitha (ରସାବଳି )is Kendrapara famous food. This place famous for lord Baldevjew Temple is known as Tulasi Kshetra and also is the most popular temple in the District. Baldevdevjew temple belongs to the 9th to 10 century AD, 

Sri Balram the elder brother of Lord Sri Krishna came to this place from the mountain Lalitgiri and killed the demon Kendarasura and married his daughter Tulasi on the full moon day of Kartika. Therefore the place is called Tulasi Khetra and is a popular tourist place in Kendrapara. Every year on the day of Kartika Purnima a large number of devotees gather here and worship lord Baldevjew. Besides this Ratha Yatra is also observed here and worships Lord Baladevjew. Besides this Rathayatra is also observed here with much pomp and ceremony. Kendrapada is famous for Rasbali which has been offered to Baladevje. Best tourist places in kendrapara Disrtict
