Kasafal Sea Beach

The famous Kasafal Sea Beach is located 45 km distance from Balasore. The cool shadows of the jungle forest with beautiful beaches attract tourists. Because it is not very deep, it is easy and safe to bathe. Every year, millions of devotees gather to bathe on the occasion of the Baruni bath. This Kasafal beach is full of red crabs & trees which is looking beautiful a real natural beauty. Kashaphal beach is a perfect fishing Centre. The sunrise and sunset at this beach are really very pleasant for the tourists. It is a very nice place to enjoy the weekend and Holiday. Kasafal Sea Beach Best Tourist Place in Balasore

Apart from Kasafal Sea Beach, Dublagadi Bagda Sea Beach, Dagara Beach, Kasafal Beach, and Chandipur Beach are the famous Sea Beach in the Balasore district. There are many hotels within a short budget for visitors near Kasafal Beach. The image of Kasafal beach is amazing to see in sunrise and sunset. Kasafala beach is located 235 km distance from Kolkata West Bengal. Kasafal Beach is one of the top attraction beaches in the Balasore district.
