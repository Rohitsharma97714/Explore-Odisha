Radha Krishna Temple

Radha Krishna Temple is located at Balaramgadi near Chandipur Sea Beach. It is about 3 km distance from Chandipur and 17 km from Balasore town. Lord Radha Krishna is worshipped in this temple. The marvellous marble statue of Lord Krishna and Radha is very attractive in this temple. 

There is a beautiful park for children to play on the temple grounds. The main attraction of the place is the large pond and a lot of colourful fish in it. Tourists can enjoy boating in the large pond. This place consists of a fish pond, Krishna temple, children’s park and rabbit house. Balaramgadi Radha Krishna mandir is one of the most famous and attractive picnic spots in Balasore, Odisha
