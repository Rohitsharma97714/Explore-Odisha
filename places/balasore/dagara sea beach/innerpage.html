Dagara Sea Beach

Dagara Sea Beach is located at a distance of 33 km from Jaleswar and about 65 km from Balasore town. A beautiful Long Beach filled with red crabs and green casuarina forest. The best time to visit Dagara Sea Beach is in the winter season. The Oceanic view of the sunset is very beautiful and also one of the cleanest beaches of Odisha. Another important attraction of the place is the Red Crabs, which can be seen on the Beach. Dagara sea beach is located 225 km distance from kolkata and 51 km from Digha Sea Beach. Dagara Beach is a popular tourist attraction places in Odisha & West bengal.

Dagra Sea Beach is a great place for swimming and surfing. There are many hotels and resorts on the Beach in Dagra, where tourists can get rest and all kinds of accommodation. The Oceanic view of the sunset is very beautiful, and also one of the cleanest beaches. It is also the best picnic spot which attracts visitors for its beautiful seashore. Dagara Beach is one of the most popular tourist places in the Balasore District. Dagra Beach has a photography point and Nature camp. There are many beaches near Dagra Beach, including Jamunasul Sea Beach, Digha Sea Beach, Kasafal Sea Beach and Bagda Beach.

Opening Time

Dagara Sea Beach Opening Time is from Monday to Sunday- Morning 6:30am to Evening 8:00pm

Best Time of visit

The best time to visit Dagara Beach is from October to June and the winter season.

Nearest Bus Stop

Nearest Railway Station

The Nearest Railway Station is Jaleswar, which is located around 41 KM distance from Dagara Beach

Nearest Airport

The nearest Airport is Biju Patnaik International Airport, Bhubaneswar, which is located around 263 KM distance from Dagara Beach
