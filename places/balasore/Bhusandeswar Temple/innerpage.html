Bhusandeswar Temple

Baba Bhusandeswar Temple is situated in Bhogarai village near the Subarnarekha River, and 36 km distance from Jaleswar and 92 km away from Balasore town. Lord Shiva is the presiding deity of this temple. The height of the Shiva-linga in this temple is 12 feet long and 14 feet wide. It is one of the largest Shiva linga in Asia. Mahashivratri is the famous festival of this temple. Throughout the year devotees from different parts of Odisha come here to worship Lord Baba Bhusandeswar. 

Baba Bhusandeswar Lingam has three parts, around 12 feet in diameter and nearly 4 feet in height. Bhusandeswar Temple is only 20 km distance from Digha Sea Beach. Bhusandeswar Shiva Temple is one of the most famous temples in Balasore, Odisha.

Opening Time

Bhusandeswar Shiva Temple opening time is Everyday Morning 6 am to Evening 7 pm

Best Time of visit

The best time to visit Bhusandeswar Shiva Temple at all times of the year.
