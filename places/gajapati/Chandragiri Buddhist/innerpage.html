Chandragiri Buddhist

Chandragiri Buddhist Monastery is located at Jirang. It is about 80 km distance from Paralakhemundi. One of the beautiful places and India’s one of the most famous Buddhist temples. In 2010 it was inaugurated by His Highness Dalai Lama. The temple is located in the eastern Ghat region of Odisha. The environment of the place is so peaceful. This peaceful place on the lap of nature has surrounded by greenery and a waterfall. 

Now Chandragiri Monastery has a modern look, and many people come to visit this place to see the Tibetan culture. There are also Jagannath Temple, Maa Kureisuni Temple in Near Chandragiri. It is the best picnic spot, and tourists come to during winter. Jirang Chandragiri Buddhist Monastery is one of the tourist places in Gajapati.
