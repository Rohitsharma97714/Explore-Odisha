Jharsuguda

Wel Come To Jharsuguda

Jharsguda is situated 40 km from Sambalpur and 372 km from the state capital Bhubaneswar. it is an industrial town and headquarter of the Jharsuguda. The district is full of mineral resources and industries. The Papermill, spinning mill, Cement factory and IB Thermal Power Plant are there. Jharsuguda is one of the popular tourist destinations. The District is decorated with many tourist places Koilighughar(Waterfall, Shiva Temple), Gujapahar Hill & Stream, Pikalghugar Stream, Vikramkhol. Best tourist places in Jharsuguda

The Jharsuguda District covers an area of 2114 sq. Km. As per the 2011 census, the total population of the District is 5,79,505 with the total male population as 2,96,690 and female population as 2,82,281. The Jharsuguda District has 1 subdivision, 358 Villages, 78 Gram Panchayats, 05 Tahasils and 05 Blocks, 3 Municipality, 1 N.A.C, 2 Assembly Constituency and 10 Police stations are there in the Jharsuguda District.
