Lal Patthar

Lal Pathar waterfall is located at Girindola, and it is 7 km from Belpahar and is 29 km from District headquarter of Jharsuguda. The waterfall is surrounded by jungle. This place is called the Lal Patthar because the side of the river is covered with red rock. Lal Pathar is one of the picnic sopt near Jharsuguda.

The water of this waterfall is flowing through the forest. Many tourists come to visit this waterfall in the winter. Lal Pathar waterfall is one of the most popular tourist places in the Jharsuguda District.
